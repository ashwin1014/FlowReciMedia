The good news is, we make any serious training content into an immersive learning experience, all customised for your organisation’s needs.

Our Online Solution

The online solution is a web-based animated, interactive and gamified video designed especially for your serious content. Our approach in making videos interactive involves a different way of storytelling and stitching it together, ensuring high audience engagement. The clickable buttons built-in the video gives the look and feel of a videogame, though these videos are much more than that

ON-OFF Solution

If you are someone who wouldn’t like to miss out on too much, we have the right combination for you. Our Product

There is no one formulae for learning how to do great sales but sitting through hours of training is not an option for human mind. It saps the energy out and learning becomes tedious and boring. To combat the problems of classroom training, we have designed “The Sales Pitch”, a board game to learn the Fundamentals of Sales. This game focuses on the philosophy of group intelligence and facilitates a learning environment conducive to harper our sales instincts. You have to play to learn the nuances of sales! In order to complement “The Sales Pitch” is our online comic-book interactive gamified video, which caters to those individuals, who like to learn when he/she has the mental space and time. We believe, this online video can make a difference to organisations in different ways.
